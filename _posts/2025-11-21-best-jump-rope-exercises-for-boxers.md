## Three Jump Rope Exercises Every Boxer Should Master

### Speed Step for Fast Feet and Rhythm

The classic boxer skip where you alternate your weight from foot to foot while keeping each jump low and snappy.
 This exercise helps you stay light and reactive, improving the quick footwork you need for angles and defensive movement.

Use it as your warmup before starting combo rounds in the app. You’ll feel your legs wake up instantly.

### Double Unders for Explosive Power

One jump, two rope rotations. Double unders teach your body to produce fast, explosive force, which carries over to sharper punches and more powerful pivots.

Try finishing a virtual boxing round with 20 seconds of double unders to spike your heart rate the same way a real exchange would.

### High-Knee Rope Running for Fight-Endurance

Lift your knees high while keeping the rope rhythm smooth. This drill hits your lungs hard and conditions your legs for sustained movement, just like bouncing in and out of range during a round.

Pair it between app combinations to simulate the push and recovery of real fight pacing.
