Free boxing round timer

If you want a simple yet effective boxing timer for free and without ads, we've got you covered on iPhone, iPad and Apple Watch. Set the number of rounds, their duration, the rest period and start!

Punching bag workouts

While all exercises work without gear, some are optimized for the heavy bag. Alternate fast punches with power strikes, improve your cardio and make using the bag much more fun!
