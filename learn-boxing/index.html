Learn boxing with an app

The Shadow Boxing App works great for both experienced and beginner boxers. If you don't have access to a gym, can't afford it or you just want to try at home before joining, learning boxing with an app is possible!

{% include getapp.html %}

Training plans

Start your boxing journey and follow training plans over a few days or a few weeks to learn boxing or improve in a particular area.

Videos to discover the techniques

The app has many bite size videos to discover the techniques required to complete the various workouts. See the techniques demonstrated by a professional boxing coach and apply them in your next training!

Detailed help

You'll also find a list of techniques, detailed descriptions and references to other videos from known boxing experts.

Record yourself

Making sure to keep proper form when learning and not getting into bad habbits is tricky. This is why we have a mirror mode that doubles as a recording feature, so that you can make sure your jab is always right.

Track your progress

Learning a new sport can be hard. To stay on track and motivated, follow your progress directly in the app or by using our Apple Health integration.

Try it for free!

Even the free version of the app offers some videos and beginner friendly workouts. There is no reason to start learning how to box today!
